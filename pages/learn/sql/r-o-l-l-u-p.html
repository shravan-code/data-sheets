Section 9: Aggregations & Grouping

ROLLUP

Base Table Context: sales

sale_id rep region amount product
1 Alice East 5000 Widget
2 Bob West 3000 Gadget
3 Alice East 7000 Gadget
4 Carol East 4000 Widget
5 Bob West 6000 Widget
6 Carol West 2000 Gadget

Generates subtotals and a grand total. Produces hierarchy of aggregations.

Sales rollup by region and product

SELECT region, product, SUM(amount) AS total FROM sales GROUP BY ROLLUP(region, product);
Output Result
region product total
East Widget 9000
East Gadget 7000
East NULL 16000
West Widget 6000
West Gadget 5000
West NULL 11000
NULL NULL 27000

NULL values represent subtotals/grand totals.
