Section 7: Filtering & Conditions

AND / OR / NOT

Base Table Context: employees

emp_id name dept_id salary manager_id
1 Alice 10 75000 NULL
2 Bob 20 60000 1
3 Carol 10 80000 1
4 Dave 20 55000 2
5 Eve 30 95000 NULL

Logical operators to combine multiple conditions.

AND — both conditions must be true

SELECT name, salary FROM employees WHERE dept_id = 10 AND salary > 70000;
Output Result
name salary
Alice 75000
Carol 80000

OR — either condition must be true

SELECT name, dept_id FROM employees WHERE dept_id = 10 OR dept_id = 30;
Output Result
name dept_id
Alice 10
Carol 10
Eve 30

NOT — negates a condition

SELECT name FROM employees WHERE NOT dept_id = 20;
Output Result
name
Alice
Carol
Eve
Pro Tips & Tricks
  • Always verify your logic with a small dataset first
  • Use EXPLAIN to see how the database handles your query
